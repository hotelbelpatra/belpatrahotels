✦ OUR STORY ✦

Sacred Food,
Sacred Land

Nestled on the main road of Orchha, steps from the divine Ram Raja Temple, Belpatra has been a pilgrims' and travellers' refuge — offering pure sattvic vegetarian meals prepared daily with devotion and the freshest local ingredients.

The Belpatra — the sacred three-leafed offering of Lord Shiva — symbolises purity, simplicity and divine connection. That spirit infuses every dish we serve and every room we offer.

✦ SACRED ORCHHA ✦

God's Own Kingdom —
Ram Raja Mandir

Orchha is home to the only temple in all of India where Lord Ram is worshipped as a King — with full royal honours, police salutes, and a state flag.

The Ram Raja Temple was originally a palace of Queen Ganeshkuwari in the 16th century. When Lord Ram's idol was brought to the temple — He refused to move. And so He was made the ruler of Orchha, a throne He holds to this day.

◆ 500+ years of uninterrupted worship
◆ Only place where Ram is worshipped as a King
◆ Just 3 minutes walk from Belpatra
