How to Reach Orchha

🚂

By Train

Nearest railway station: Jhansi Junction (17 km). Regular trains from Delhi, Bhopal, Agra. Taxi/auto from Jhansi to Orchha takes 30 min.

🚗

By Road

Orchha is 17 km from Jhansi, 165 km from Gwalior, 230 km from Bhopal. Well-connected by NH 75. Cabs available from all major cities.

✈️

By Air

Nearest airport: Gwalior Airport (133 km) or Khajuraho Airport (175 km). Taxi to Orchha available from both.
