About Belpatra

A story of devotion, purity and hospitality rooted in the sacred soil of Orchha

✦ THE BELPATRA LEAF ✦

Named After a
Sacred Offering

The Belpatra — the three-leafed offering sacred to Lord Shiva — represents purity, simplicity and the divine connection between the devotee and the creator. It is with this spirit that we named our establishment.

Founded on the main road of Orchha, steps from the divine Ram Raja Temple, Belpatra began as a humble kitchen serving pilgrims and travellers with freshly cooked, sattvic vegetarian meals. Word of mouth grew our reputation, and soon we expanded to offer comfortable heritage accommodation as well.

Today, with over 1400 Google reviews and a 4.8-star rating, Belpatra stands as Orchha's most-loved pure vegetarian destination — a home away from home for pilgrims, tourists and food lovers alike.
